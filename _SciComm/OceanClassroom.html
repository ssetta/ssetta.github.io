--- title: "URI Narragansett Bay Classroom" excerpt: "Outreach Scientist for Ocean Classroom
" collection: SciComm --- Outreach Scientist for Ocean Classroom (Narragansett Bay) focused on communicating ocean science by either a classroom visit or field trip to sites around Narragansett Bay. Outreach scientists focused on communicating core concepts in oceanography to elementary and middle school aged kids from schools across Rhode Island.
